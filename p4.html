Exercise 2-- Modular 3d Oragami

Funny story to tell, one of my familiy members used to make these oragami things while I was kid. She had a big baeutiful swang made with these things and I liked it a lot. Back then I wonder how did she make such an impressive piece with such a strong structure which can easily support its big size.
Now with some self-experience, I figure out that each of these oragami things are working as a small module, and by connecting them with each other we can build something really strong. However, it really takes some skills to find the correct way to insert one into another and try to keep them that way only with friction force.
